Overnight Oats

Descrption

Overnight oats are quick and easy to make while also being delicious and very filling.

Follow the easy steps bellow to make yours!

Ingredients

  • Oats (Any type, though steel-cut works best
  • Milk (again any type)
  • Toppings of choice (Fruit, chocolate, anything really)

Steps

  1. Put 50 grams of oats in a container
  2. Top it with 150 grams of milk of your choice (or triple the amount of oats, if scaling this recipe)
  3. (Optional) Adding your fruit or toppings and mix them in
  4. Put a lid or cover and leave it in the fridge over night!
