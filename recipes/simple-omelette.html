Simple omelette

Descrption

Fluffy, quick and easy to make

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Turn the heat on to medium high and heat up your pan
  2. Put some butter in it and swirl it around so it covers the whole surface
  3. Crack your eggs in a bowl and beat them until smooty
  4. Add salt and pepper in your beaten eggs
  5. Pour the mixture into your pan
  6. Gently move the pan around so the whole surface is covered
  7. Wait until the omelette firms up a bit and then use a spatula to fold it
  8. Serve and enjoy!
