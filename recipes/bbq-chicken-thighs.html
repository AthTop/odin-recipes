BBQ Chicken Thighs

Descrption

Juicy and delicious chicken thighs grilled on the barbecue

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Seasonings
  • Olive Oil or Mustard

Steps

  1. Prepare your thighs by massaging your olive oil or mustard on them to help bind the seasonings
  2. Apply your seasonings of choice on your thighs
  3. Grill on the BBQ until ready (Use an internal thermometer if unsure)
  4. That's it! Let them rest for 5 minutes before eating!
